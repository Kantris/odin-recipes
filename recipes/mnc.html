Mac And Cheese

Description

This is a bare-bones method for making some very cheesey veleta(sp) mac and cheese. Go hog wild.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw elbow macaroni
  • 6 oz. diced Velveta(sp) cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Boil 1 cup macaroni for 8 minutes
  3. Strain macaroni
  4. Reduce 6 oz. diced Velveta cheese in 1/4 cup milk on low heat
  5. Add macaroni, stir well
  6. Salt and Pepper to taste
